Creates a bone with an id and a name. A bone should typically be defined pointing away from the root or parent.

Please note that the id is used by the FL* sequence generator to identify the column of the subsequence that is addressed by the bone. The id can and should be negative to distinguish left and right side. The left side is typically the negative side.

Parameters
idThe bone's id. Please note that the reserved values 9999 and 10000 should not be used.
nameThe bone's name.
fromThis bone is ranging from the joint with this id.
toThis bone is ranging to the joint with this id.

Creates a bone that has a middle joint with an ID and a name. A bone should typically be defined pointing away from the root or parent.

Please note that the id is used by the FL* sequence generator to identify the column of the subsequence that is addressed by the bone. The id can and should be negative to distinguish left and right side. The left side is typically the negative side.

Parameters
idThe bone's id. Please note that the reserved values 9999 and 10000 should not be used.
nameThe bone's name.
fromhis bone is ranging from the joint with this id.
tohis bone is ranging to the joint with this id.
middle_jointJoint lying between the two outer ones (e.g. the elbow for from shoulder to hand).
