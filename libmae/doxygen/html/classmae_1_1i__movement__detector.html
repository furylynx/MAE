Detects movement in the streamed skeleton sequence for the given body parts. Therefore, a buffer is required and the whole movement sequence for the buffer is returned.

Parameters
timestampThe timestamp of the current frame as provided by the user. It is not save to rely on this value.
framerateThe framerate to be applied. The framerate does not change.
skeletonThe skeleton data.
body_partsThe addressed body parts.
Returns
The detected movement sequence.
