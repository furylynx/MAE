Creates a new movement controller with controllers for the steps of the processing chain. The movement detector is built from the pose detector and the sequence generator. A key pose approach is used to do this.

Parameters
ipdThe pose detector to detect poses from the stream.
isgThe sequence generator to generate sequences out of pose sequences.
isrThe sequence recognizer to recognize sequences.
body_partsThe addressed body parts.
pose_buffer_sizeThe initial pose buffer size.
framerateThe framerate of the skeleton stream.
debugTrue for debug output on the terminal.
